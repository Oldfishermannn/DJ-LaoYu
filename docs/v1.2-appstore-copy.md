## 3. Description（完整版）

```
Simone is an AI mood radio. Tune a mood, and let it play.

Press play and a station tunes itself — a Lo-fi tape spool turning on a quiet afternoon, a Jazz room at closing time, an R&B lounge with the decanter still out, a Rock hearth burning low, an Electronic city skyline at dusk. No playlists. No repeats. No ads. Just instrumental music, generated in real time by Google's Lyria AI, for the mood you're already in.

This isn't a music player. It's a radio. Pick a channel, let it drift.

— WHAT YOU GET —

Five stations, always on
Lo-fi · Jazz · R&B · Rock · Electronic. Each station holds dozens of sub-styles inside — from Bossa Nova and Neo Soul to Synthwave and Post Rock. Swipe sideways to change station. Tap ◁ ▷ to move through styles inside it.

Evolve — quiet drift, not jump cuts
The music subtly reshapes itself over time. Instruments come and go, density shifts, energy breathes. It never suddenly switches genre on you. Set the pace: 30s, 1m, 5m, or Lock.

A visualizer for every station
Each station carries its own visual weather — a tape spool, a green oscilloscope line, a rippling decanter, a flickering hearth, a city skyline at dusk. The picture moves with the music.

Sleep timer, background play, Now Playing artwork
Keeps going when you lock the screen. Shows up properly on the lock screen and in Control Center.

Fog City Nocturne — a quiet visual language
Cool OKLCH palette. Unbounded, Fraunces, Archivo typography. Settings fit on one calm screen. Built to stay out of the music's way.

Free, with your own key when you want more
Ships with a built-in trial key so you can just open it and listen. Bring your own Gemini API key from Google AI Studio for unlimited use. Your key lives in the iOS Keychain, never leaves your device.

— HOW PEOPLE USE IT —

• Deep focus — Study Beats, Modal Jazz, or Minimal Techno while you work.
• The drive — Synthwave, Post Rock, or Downtempo for long roads.
• The room — Smooth Jazz, Neo Soul, or Bossa Nova as the background of a quiet evening at home.
• Get-together — Chill Pop, Funky Soul, or Indie Rock at low volume behind conversation.
• Winding down — Lo-fi Rain, Jazz Ballad, or Quiet Storm with the sleep timer on.

— WHY IT EXISTS —

Most music apps are shelves: rows of albums, playlists, recommendations, decisions. Simone is the opposite. It's what plays in the background of a good hour — the desk, the passenger seat, the kitchen, the couch. You don't pick a song. You pick a mood, and it unfolds.

Because trust is the prerequisite for simplicity. Press play. Simone takes care of the rest.

— PRIVACY —

No accounts. No tracking. No analytics. Your API key is stored in the iOS Keychain on your device. Simone connects directly to Google's Lyria API — nothing routes through our servers, because there are no servers.

— NOTES —

• Music is generated live, so an internet connection is required.
• The built-in trial key is shared and rate-limited; for heavy listening, add your own key in Settings.
• Lyria is an experimental AI model by Google. Occasional instrumental quirks are part of the territory — treat them as weather.

A calm radio, for any hour.
```
